  3. Take a right turn immediately after you drive past the guard shack (10 feet) on to South Drive.
  4. There are about 50, 1-hour parking spaces (on South Drive) in the front of the 100 building. Once you park, use the “100 L” entrance (there are two front entrances “L” and “E”, again, take “100 L”).
